Positive Impacts

This new wave of personal assistant allows consumers to complete tasks easier and faster than before. The ability to order new items off of Amazon just by asking Alexa for it has led consumers to buy more online, in the comfort of their own home. In turn, online shopping has become even more popular than it was before.

Negative Impacts

The Amazon Alexa is always listening (unless the mute button is on) to conversations after a user says the name 'Alexa' and sometimes even if her name isn't said aloud. This slightly creepy aspect is only further fuelled by the fact that alexa sends recordings of her conversations back to Amazon. This has caused many owners of Alexas to become sceptical of their personal assistants and Amazon themselves. Furthermore, if there was a security breach in Amazon, hackers would have access to this personal information of all of these user, and this could eventually lead Amazon's stocks to go down due to loss of trust in the company.
